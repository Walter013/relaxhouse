The Restaurant

Escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and indulge in a truly serene dining experience at Relax House Restaurant. Nestled in the heart of Thailand, our relaxed restaurant invites you to unwind in a tranquil oasis of flavor and culture.

Quench your thirst with a selection of handcrafted cocktails, exotic fruit juices, and locally sourced beverages. Our drink menu complements the flavors of our cuisine, ensuring a complete and satisfying dining experience.

The Bungalows

Nestled in the heart of this serene island, our bungalow offers the ultimate tropical retreat. Wake up to the soothing sounds of waves lapping at the shore, surrounded by lush greenery and crystal-clear waters.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a peaceful solo retreat, or quality time with family and friends, our Tranquil Bungalow is your haven of tranquility in Koh Kood.
